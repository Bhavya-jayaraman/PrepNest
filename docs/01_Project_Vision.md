# 1. Introduction

PrepNest is an AI-powered career preparation platform designed to help students prepare for placements, internships, and technical interviews through realistic AI-driven interview simulations, coding assessments, resume analysis, and personalized career guidance.

Unlike traditional interview preparation platforms that focus on only one aspect of preparation, PrepNest provides a complete ecosystem where students can practice, learn, improve, and track their interview readiness in one place.

The platform aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by offering an interactive and personalized interview experience powered by Artificial Intelligence.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

To become the most trusted AI-powered career preparation platform that empowers students to confidently prepare for interviews, improve their technical and communication skills, and achieve their career goals.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

To make interview preparation accessible, personalized, and realistic by leveraging Artificial Intelligence to simulate real interview experiences, provide meaningful feedback, and guide students throughout their placement journey.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Every year, thousands of students struggle during placements because they lack structured interview preparation.

Common challenges include:

- Limited exposure to real interview environments.
- Lack of confidence while answering questions.
- Difficulty identifying weak technical concepts.
- No personalized feedback after practicing.
- Multiple platforms are required for coding, interviews, resume review, and progress tracking.

As a result, students often enter interviews without knowing what to expect or how to improve.

---

# 5. Proposed Solution

PrepNest addresses these challenges by providing an all-in-one AI-powered career preparation platform.

The platform enables students to:

- Participate in realistic AI-powered mock interviews.
- Practice technical, HR, and coding interviews.
- Receive personalized AI-generated feedback.
- Upload resumes for AI-based review.
- Monitor interview performance over time.
- Follow personalized learning recommendations.
- Prepare for company-specific interview patterns.

PrepNest acts as a personal AI mentor, helping students continuously improve until they are interview-ready.
